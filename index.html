Infrastructure & Facilities

Nurture International School – A lively space that facilitates learning at one’s pace, style and passion.

The eco friendly campus at 9th Mile, Bone Mill, Hessarghatta main road with wider accessibility ensures a productive learning experience for your children.

campus

A serene 4-acre school campus nestled in lush greenery, good connectivity providing a pleasant and safe learning environment for the budding all rounders.

Classrooms

Our spacious, well-lit and naturally ventilated classrooms foster an ideal learning environment for the young seekers. Experience education with safety and comfort.

Safety & Security

Rest easy, your child is safeguarded through 24x7 robust digital and manual surveillance systems, ensuring a secure and nurturing school environment.

Transport

Our school transport system with a female attendant offers a secure, efficient, and meticulously planned route, ensuring your child's safety and punctuality.

Science Labs

Our well-equipped laboratories provide individual learning and experimentation time to the students to develop a high order of practical skills.

Coding and Robotics lab

Our coding and robotics lab foster problem solving skills. Nurture technical and analyzing skills..

Library

Our expansive, well-stocked library inculcates the love for reading and learning, provides an inspiring haven for curious minds.

Performing arts room

The spacious and well designed performing arts room aids the aspiring students to embrace their hidden talents .

Integrated sports curriculum

Our well designed integrated sports curriculum supported by state of the art sports complex and amenities pave way for a sporting success.

Toddlers play pen

Discover our thoughtfully designed, separate play areas tailored to your child's needs, ensuring safety, engagement, and joyful experiences.
